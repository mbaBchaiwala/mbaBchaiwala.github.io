Let’s talk about Chai

In India, chai is more than just a cup of tea to start the day - the thick sweet drink is an integral part of the rhythm of life.
The monsoon clouds on the horizon and the hot cup of tea in hand have always mesmerised me beyond description.

Ahhhh, Samosa

The ever-popular chai-samosa is the pairing of tea and samosa, something that unites the country in enjoyment. Many a great conversation, whether over lunch or late into the night, has been shared over this cultural pastime.
